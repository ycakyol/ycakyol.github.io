        Each city in Turkey has its own traditional dishes and of course Amasya is one of them. the city has a very rich cuisine because it is located in the black sea.The dishes are not very heavy but also they are not very light,too. Amasya people use rarely meat in their local dishes. Now I am going to show you most delicius local dishes of Amasya. I hope you are not hungry.


                          APPLE



         First of all of course we will start with apple.The city of Amasya most famous for apple before the food.Apple becomes a sign for Amasya and it is very important for Amasya.    The apple of Amasya is sweater and smaller than other apple also it has beatiful a red color. Amasya's apple is eaten and loved all over the country.
